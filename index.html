Kevin De Broyer

Kevin is an Ghent-based painter. He is studying in Luca School Of Arts in Ghent. 
His work tends to fall into 3 categories: abstract paintings, Landscape paintings and Goofing around paintings.
His work is on display in various places in Belgium like his home and homes of his friends.
